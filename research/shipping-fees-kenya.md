## Additional Costs to Consider

### 1. Customs & Import Duties
- **Art & Handicrafts**: 0-25% depending on item and destination
- **Textiles**: 5-20%
- **Jewelry**: 5-30%
- **Wood products**: 5-15%

### 2. Insurance
- Recommended: 1-3% of declared value
- Coverage: Loss, damage, theft

### 3. Fuel Surcharges
- Current average: 15-25% of shipping cost
- Subject to monthly changes

### 4. Remote Area Surcharges
- Additional $15-35 for remote destinations

### 5. Documentation Fees
- Commercial Invoice: $10-25
- Certificate of Origin: $15-30

## Recommended Shipping Partners for ArtAfrik

### For Small Packages (Under 2kg)
1. **EMS** - Best value for budget shipping
2. **DHL Express** - Best for speed and tracking

### For Medium Packages (2-20kg)
1. **DHL Express** - Best balance of speed and cost
2. **FedEx** - Good for USA and Europe
3. **UPS** - Excellent for business shipments

### For Large/Heavy Items (20kg+)
1. **Freight Forwarders** - Contact local freight companies
2. **Sea Freight** - Cost-effective for bulk orders
3. **Air Freight** - For urgent large shipments
